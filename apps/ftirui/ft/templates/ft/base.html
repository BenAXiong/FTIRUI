    mlirui experience

    Plot, collaborate, and report without friction.

    Everything you need to explore FT-IR data, share context, and move projects forward, packed into a calm, browser-first workspace.

    • Plot graphs, hassle-free

      Drag, drop, and fine-tune spectra with auto-scaling, comparison overlays, and publication-ready themes.

    • Extend with ease

      Modular architecture welcomes custom plugins, ML helpers, or scripting pipelines that match your lab.

    • Share projects with labmates

      .ben workspaces sync through the cloud so teammates can pick up exactly where you left off.

    • Collaborate on papers

      Inline annotations and citations keep multidisciplinary authors aligned from draft to final submission.

    • Connect to instruments

      Stream fresh spectra straight into your plots with secure API bridges to FT-IR hardware.

    • Export publication-ready graphs

      High-DPI PNG, SVG, or vector exports that drop neatly into manuscripts and slide decks.

    • Instant reports

      One-click templates summarize runs, attach spectra, and deliver routine experimental reports in minutes.
