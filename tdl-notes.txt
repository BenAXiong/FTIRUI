Peak finding tech notes:
- Baseline/integration/smoothing interplay: smoothing is tightly coupled with peak detection 
    because it dictates S/N; baseline correction should be optional but encouraged before detection 
    on strongly sloped spectra; integration is downstream (peaks found here can be passed 
    to the integration feature but should stay decoupled so users can detect peaks without integrating).
- Alternatives/future enhancements: consider FFT-based smoothing or continuous wavelet transforms 
    for more robust multi-scale peak finding, allow per-trace overrides (different sensitivity per trace), 
    expose peak templates (e.g., Gaussian vs. Lorentzian fits), or integrate a “reference library” cross-check 
    where detected peaks are immediately compared to known band positions.
